Farming Tips

Before planting, conduct a soil test to understand nutrient levels. Healthy soil is the foundation of a productive farm.

Use compost, organic manure, and cover crops to improve soil structure and fertility.

Rotate crops to prevent soil depletion and manage pests/diseases naturally.
